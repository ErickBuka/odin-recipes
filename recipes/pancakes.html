Pancakes

Fluffy homemade pancakes perfect for breakfast.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • Pinch of salt
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tbsp melted butter

Instructions

  1. Mix flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt.
  2. In another bowl, whisk milk, egg, and melted butter.
  3. Combine wet and dry ingredients. Do not overmix.
  4. Heat a skillet and pour batter for each pancake.
  5. Cook until bubbles form, flip and cook until golden.
  6. Serve warm with syrup.
