Banana Bread

This classic banana bread is moist, delicious, and easy to make. Perfect for breakfast or a snack!

Ingredients

  • 3 ripe bananas
  • 1/2 cup melted butter
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • Pinch of salt
  • 1.5 cups all-purpose flour

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease a loaf pan.
  2. Mash bananas in a bowl. Mix in melted butter.
  3. Add sugar, egg, and vanilla. Mix well.
  4. Sprinkle baking soda and salt, mix in.
  5. Add flour and mix until just combined.
  6. Pour into loaf pan and bake for 50-60 minutes.
  7. Let cool before slicing. Enjoy!
