Crepes

Crepes are thin pancakes that can be filled with a variety of sweet or savory fillings. They are a versatile dish that can be enjoyed for breakfast, dessert, or even as a main course.

Light, thin, and delicious French-style pancakes perfect for breakfast or dessert.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 2 tbsp melted butter
  • 1/4 tsp salt

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, whisk together flour and eggs.
  2. Gradually add milk and water, stirring to combine.
  3. Add melted butter and salt; mix until smooth.
  4. Heat a lightly oiled skillet over medium-high heat.
  5. Pour about 1/4 cup of batter into the pan, tilting to coat evenly.
  6. Cook for 1-2 minutes, flip and cook the other side until golden.
  7. Serve warm with your favorite fillings or toppings.
