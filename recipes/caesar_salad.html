Caesar Salad

Description

A classic Caesar salad with crisp romaine lettuce, homemade croutons, and creamy Caesar dressing.

Ingredients

  • 2 heads romaine lettuce, chopped
  • 1 cup croutons
  • 1/2 cup Parmesan cheese, shaved
  • For the dressing:
  • 1/2 cup mayonnaise
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon Dijon mustard
  • 1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • Salt and black pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Wash and dry lettuce thoroughly
  2. Make the dressing by combining all dressing ingredients
  3. Whisk dressing until smooth
  4. In a large bowl, toss lettuce with dressing
  5. Add croutons
  6. Top with shaved Parmesan
  7. Season with fresh black pepper
  8. Serve immediately
