Omelette

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • Optional fillings: cheese, vegetables, ham, etc.

Instructions

  1. Crack the eggs into a bowl and whisk them until well combined.
  2. Season with salt and pepper.
  3. Heat the butter in a non-stick skillet over medium heat.
  4. Pour the eggs into the skillet and let them cook for about 1-2 minutes, or until the edges start to set.
  5. Using a spatula, gently lift the edges of the omelette and tilt the skillet to allow uncooked eggs to flow to the edges.
  6. When the omelette is mostly set but still slightly runny on top, add your desired fillings on one half.
  7. Fold the omelette in half over the fillings.
  8. Cook for another minute or until the omelette is fully set and the cheese (if used) is melted.
  9. Slide the omelette onto a plate and serve hot.

Enjoy your delicious omelette!
