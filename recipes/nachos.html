Loaded Nachos

Description

Crispy tortilla chips loaded with melted cheese, seasoned beef, and all your favorite toppings. Perfect for game day, parties, or as a shared appetizer!

Ingredients

  • Base:
  • 1 large bag tortilla chips
  • 2 cups shredded Mexican cheese blend
  • Meat Layer:
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 packet taco seasoning
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Toppings:
  • 1 can black beans, drained
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 jalapeño, sliced
  • 1/2 cup black olives, sliced
  • 1/2 red onion, diced
  • 2 green onions, chopped
  • For Serving:
  • Sour cream
  • Guacamole
  • Fresh salsa
  • Sliced jalapeños
  • Fresh cilantro
  • Lime wedges

Steps

  1. Prepare the Meat:
  2. Brown ground beef in a large skillet
  3. Add diced onion and garlic
  4. Stir in taco seasoning and water
  5. Simmer until sauce thickens
  6. Assembly:
  7. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C)
  8. Layer tortilla chips on a large baking sheet
  9. Top with seasoned beef
  10. Add black beans
  11. Sprinkle generous amount of cheese
  12. Add jalapeños and olives
  13. Baking:
  14. Bake for 10-15 minutes until cheese melts
  15. Remove from oven
  16. Final Touches:
  17. Top with fresh tomatoes
  18. Add diced red and green onions
  19. Garnish with cilantro
  20. Serve with accompaniments

Tips

  • Use thick, sturdy tortilla chips
  • Layer ingredients evenly
  • Don't overcrowd toppings
  • Serve immediately while cheese is hot
  • Place wet toppings on the side to prevent soggy chips

Variations

  • Use shredded chicken instead of beef
  • Make it vegetarian with extra beans and vegetables
  • Add corn, bell peppers, or other vegetables
  • Use different cheese combinations
  • Add refried beans as a base layer
