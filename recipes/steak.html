Perfect Steak

Description

Learn how to cook the perfect steak with a beautiful crust and juicy center.

Ingredients

  • 2 ribeye steaks (1-inch thick)
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 4 garlic cloves
  • Fresh rosemary and thyme
  • Coarse salt
  • Fresh ground black pepper

Steps

  1. Bring steaks to room temperature
  2. Pat steaks dry with paper towels
  3. Season generously with salt and pepper
  4. Heat cast iron skillet until very hot
  5. Add oil and sear steaks 4-5 minutes per side
  6. Add butter, garlic, and herbs
  7. Baste steaks with butter
  8. Rest for 5-10 minutes before serving
