Classic Spaghetti

Description

A classic Italian spaghetti with rich meat sauce and fresh herbs.

Ingredients

  • 1 package spaghetti
  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 cans crushed tomatoes
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 1 tablespoon Italian seasoning
  • Fresh basil
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Cook spaghetti according to package
  2. Brown meat with onions and garlic
  3. Add tomatoes and seasonings
  4. Simmer sauce for 30 minutes
  5. Drain pasta
  6. Combine pasta and sauce
  7. Top with fresh basil and Parmesan
