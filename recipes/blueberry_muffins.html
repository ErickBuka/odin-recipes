Blueberry Muffins

These blueberry muffins are soft, fluffy, and bursting with fresh blueberries.

Ingredients

  • 1.5 cups all-purpose flour
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • 1/3 cup vegetable oil
  • 1 egg
  • 1/3 cup milk
  • 1 cup fresh blueberries

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C). Line muffin tin with papers.
  2. Mix flour, sugar, salt, and baking powder.
  3. In another bowl, mix oil, egg, and milk.
  4. Combine wet and dry ingredients. Fold in blueberries.
  5. Fill muffin cups and bake for 20-25 minutes.
  6. Let cool and enjoy!
