Egg Salad Sandwich

Description

A creamy and delicious egg salad sandwich that's perfect for lunch or picnics.

Ingredients

  • 6 hard-boiled eggs, chopped
  • 1/4 cup mayonnaise
  • 1 celery stalk, finely diced
  • 2 tablespoons red onion, finely diced
  • 1 teaspoon Dijon mustard
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 8 slices of bread
  • Lettuce leaves

Steps

  1. Peel and chop the hard-boiled eggs
  2. In a bowl, mix eggs, mayonnaise, celery, and onion
  3. Add Dijon mustard
  4. Season with salt and pepper
  5. Mix well until combined
  6. Toast the bread if desired
  7. Place lettuce on bread slices
  8. Spread egg mixture on bread
  9. Close sandwich and serve
