Vegetable Stir Fry

Description

A quick and healthy vegetable stir fry with a flavorful sauce, perfect for a light lunch.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups mixed vegetables (broccoli, carrots, snap peas, bell peppers)
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 inch ginger, grated
  • 3 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch
  • 1/4 cup water
  • Sesame seeds for garnish

Steps

  1. Cut all vegetables into similar-sized pieces
  2. Mix soy sauce, sesame oil, cornstarch, and water in a bowl
  3. Heat vegetable oil in a wok or large pan
  4. Add garlic and ginger, stir-fry for 30 seconds
  5. Add vegetables, starting with the firmest ones
  6. Stir-fry for 5-7 minutes
  7. Pour in sauce mixture
  8. Cook until sauce thickens
  9. Garnish with sesame seeds and serve
