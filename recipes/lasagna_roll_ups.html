Lasagna Roll-Ups

Description

A fun twist on traditional lasagna, these roll-ups are perfect for portion control and presentation.

Ingredients

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 15 oz ricotta cheese
  • 1 cup mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup Parmesan cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 2 cups marinara sauce
  • 2 cups spinach, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Italian seasoning
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C)
  2. Cook lasagna noodles until all done
  3. Mix ricotta, spinach, egg, and seasonings
  4. Spread mixture on each noodle
  5. Roll up each noodle tightly
  6. Place rolls in baking dish
  7. Top with marinara and cheese
  8. Bake for 30 minutes until bubbly
