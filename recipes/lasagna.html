Classic Lasagna

Description

A rich and hearty Italian classic with layers of pasta, meat sauce, and three types of cheese.

Ingredients

  • 1 package lasagna noodles
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 pound Italian sausage
  • 2 cans crushed tomatoes
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups mozzarella cheese
  • 1 cup Parmesan cheese
  • 2 eggs
  • Italian herbs and seasonings
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C)
  2. Cook lasagna noodles according to package directions
  3. Brown meat with onions and garlic
  4. Add tomatoes and seasonings to make sauce
  5. Mix ricotta, eggs, and Parmesan
  6. Layer: sauce, noodles, ricotta mix, mozzarella
  7. Repeat layers 3 times
  8. Cover with foil and bake 25 minutes
  9. Uncover and bake 25 more minutes
  10. Let rest 15 minutes before serving
