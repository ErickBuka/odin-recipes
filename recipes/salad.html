Garden Fresh Salad

Description

A refreshing and healthy garden salad packed with fresh vegetables and a light vinaigrette dressing.

Ingredients

  • Mixed salad greens
  • 1 cucumber, sliced
  • 2 tomatoes, wedged
  • 1 red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 bell pepper, sliced
  • 1/2 cup olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons balsamic vinegar
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional: croutons

Steps

  1. Wash and dry all vegetables thoroughly
  2. In a large bowl, combine the mixed greens
  3. Add cucumber, tomatoes, onion, and bell pepper
  4. In a small bowl, whisk together olive oil and balsamic vinegar
  5. Season the dressing with salt and pepper
  6. Drizzle the dressing over the salad
  7. Toss gently to combine
  8. Top with croutons if desired
  9. Serve immediately
