Basic Soup

Description

A versatile and comforting soup recipe that can be customized with your favorite ingredients.

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 carrots, sliced
  • 2 celery stalks, sliced
  • 8 cups chicken or vegetable broth
  • 1 cup pasta or rice
  • 2 cups mixed vegetables
  • Fresh herbs
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Heat oil in large pot
  2. Saut√© onion, carrots, and celery
  3. Add broth and bring to boil
  4. Add pasta or rice
  5. Add vegetables
  6. Simmer until tender
  7. Season with herbs, salt, and pepper
