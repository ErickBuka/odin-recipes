Perfect Roast Chicken

Description

A classic roast chicken with crispy skin and juicy meat, perfect for any special dinner.

Ingredients

  • 1 whole chicken (4-5 lbs)
  • 2 tablespoons butter, softened
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 lemon
  • Fresh herbs (thyme, rosemary)
  • Salt and pepper
  • 1 onion, quartered
  • 2 carrots, chunked
  • 2 celery stalks, chunked

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 425°F (220°C)
  2. Pat chicken dry with paper towels
  3. Mix butter with garlic and herbs
  4. Season cavity with salt and pepper
  5. Stuff with lemon and herbs
  6. Rub butter mixture under and over skin
  7. Surround with vegetables
  8. Roast for 1 hour and 15 minutes
  9. Rest for 15 minutes before carving
