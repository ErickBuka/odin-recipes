Fresh Fruit Salad

Description

A refreshing and colorful fruit salad that's perfect for a light lunch or side dish.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups strawberries, halved
  • 2 oranges, segmented
  • 1 cup grapes
  • 2 bananas, sliced
  • 1 apple, diced
  • 1 cup blueberries
  • 1 mango, diced
  • 2 tablespoons honey
  • 1 lime, juiced
  • Fresh mint for garnish

Steps

  1. Wash all fruit thoroughly
  2. Cut fruit into bite-sized pieces
  3. Combine all fruit in a large bowl
  4. Mix honey and lime juice
  5. Drizzle honey-lime mixture over fruit
  6. Gently toss to combine
  7. Garnish with fresh mint
  8. Chill for 30 minutes before serving
