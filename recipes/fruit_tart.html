Fresh Fruit Tart

Description

A beautiful and delicious fresh fruit tart with a buttery crust, smooth pastry cream, and topped with glazed fresh fruits. Perfect for special occasions or when you want to impress!

Ingredients

  • For the Tart Crust (Pâte Sucrée):
  • 1 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup powdered sugar
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup cold butter, cubed
  • 1 large egg yolk
  • 2-3 tablespoons cold water
  • For the Pastry Cream:
  • 2 cups whole milk
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 4 egg yolks
  • 1/4 cup cornstarch
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • For the Topping:
  • 2 cups mixed fresh fruits (strawberries, kiwi, raspberries, blueberries)
  • 1/4 cup apricot jam
  • 1 tablespoon water

Steps

  1. For the Tart Crust:
  2. Mix flour, sugar, and salt in food processor
  3. Pulse in cold butter until crumbly
  4. Add egg yolk and water until dough forms
  5. Chill dough for 1 hour
  6. Roll out and place in tart pan
  7. Blind bake at 375°F for 20-25 minutes
  8. Cool completely
  9. For the Pastry Cream:
  10. Heat milk until almost boiling
  11. Whisk egg yolks, sugar, and cornstarch
  12. Slowly add hot milk to egg mixture
  13. Return to pan and cook until thickened
  14. Stir in vanilla and butter
  15. Strain and chill completely
  16. Assembly:
  17. Spread pastry cream in cooled tart shell
  18. Arrange fresh fruits on top
  19. Heat jam with water
  20. Brush glaze over fruits
  21. Chill until ready to serve

Tips

  • Crust can be made a day ahead
  • Pastry cream should be very cold before filling
  • Pat fruits dry before arranging
  • Best served the same day
