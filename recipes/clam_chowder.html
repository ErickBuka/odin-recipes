New England Clam Chowder

Description

A creamy and rich New England-style clam chowder with tender potatoes and fresh clams.

Ingredients

  • 4 slices bacon, diced
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 3 potatoes, cubed
  • 2 cans chopped clams with juice
  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 2 cups clam juice
  • 1/4 cup flour
  • 2 bay leaves
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Oyster crackers for serving

Steps

  1. Cook bacon until crispy
  2. Saut√© onions in bacon fat
  3. Add flour to make roux
  4. Add clam juice and potatoes
  5. Simmer until potatoes are tender
  6. Add cream and clams
  7. Heat through without boiling
  8. Serve with crackers
