Hearty Vegetable Soup

Description

A warming vegetable soup packed with nutrients and flavor.

Ingredients

  • 2 onions, diced
  • 3 carrots, chopped
  • 3 celery stalks, chopped
  • 2 potatoes, cubed
  • 2 cups green beans
  • 2 cups cabbage, chopped
  • 2 cans diced tomatoes
  • 8 cups vegetable broth
  • 2 bay leaves
  • Italian seasoning
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Saut√© onions, carrots, and celery
  2. Add potatoes and green beans
  3. Pour in broth and tomatoes
  4. Add seasonings and bay leaves
  5. Simmer for 20 minutes
  6. Add cabbage
  7. Cook additional 10 minutes
  8. Season to taste and serve
