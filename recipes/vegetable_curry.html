Vegetable Curry

Description

A flavorful and aromatic vegetable curry that's both healthy and satisfying.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups mixed vegetables (potatoes, carrots, peas, cauliflower)
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons curry powder
  • 1 can coconut milk
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 inch ginger, grated
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a large pot
  2. Saut√© onions until translucent
  3. Add garlic and ginger, cook for 1 minute
  4. Add curry powder and stir
  5. Add vegetables and tomatoes
  6. Pour in coconut milk
  7. Simmer for 20-25 minutes
  8. Season with salt
  9. Garnish with cilantro and serve
