Peanut Butter Cookies

Description

Classic peanut butter cookies with the signature fork cross-hatch pattern - crispy on the outside, chewy on the inside!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 cup creamy peanut butter
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 cup packed brown sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons baking soda
  • Extra sugar for rolling

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C)
  2. Cream together butter and peanut butter
  3. Add sugars and beat until fluffy
  4. Beat in eggs one at a time
  5. Mix dry ingredients separately
  6. Combine wet and dry ingredients
  7. Roll dough into 1-inch balls
  8. Roll balls in sugar
  9. Place on ungreased cookie sheets
  10. Make criss-cross pattern with fork
  11. Bake for 10-12 minutes
  12. Cool on wire racks
