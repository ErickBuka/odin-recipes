Classic Burger

Description

A juicy, perfectly seasoned burger with all the classic toppings - perfect for a quick meal or casual entertaining!

Ingredients

  • For the patties:
  • 1 pound ground beef (80/20)
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
  • For assembly:
  • 4 hamburger buns
  • 4 slices cheese (optional)
  • Lettuce leaves
  • Tomato slices
  • Red onion slices
  • Pickles
  • Mayonnaise
  • Ketchup
  • Mustard

Steps

  1. Mix ground beef with seasonings
  2. Form into 4 equal-sized patties
  3. Make a thumb impression in center
  4. Heat grill or skillet to medium-high
  5. Cook patties 4-5 minutes per side
  6. Add cheese if desired
  7. Toast the buns
  8. Assemble burgers:
  9. - Spread condiments on buns
  10. - Add lettuce and tomato
  11. - Place patty on vegetables
  12. - Top with onion and pickles
  13. Serve immediately
