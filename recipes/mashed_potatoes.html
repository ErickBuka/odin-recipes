Creamy Mashed Potatoes

Description

Smooth and creamy mashed potatoes, the perfect side dish for any meal.

Ingredients

  • 4 lbs Yukon Gold potatoes
  • 1 cup butter
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 4 cloves roasted garlic (optional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Chives for garnish

Steps

  1. Peel and cut potatoes
  2. Boil in salted water until tender
  3. Drain potatoes well
  4. Heat milk and cream
  5. Mash potatoes
  6. Add butter and warm milk mixture
  7. Season to taste
  8. Garnish with chives
