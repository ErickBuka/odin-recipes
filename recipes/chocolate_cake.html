Chocolate Cake

Description

A rich, moist chocolate cake with smooth chocolate frosting - perfect for any celebration or when you need a chocolate fix!

Ingredients

  • For the cake:
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups sugar
  • 3/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 2 teaspoons baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1 cup hot coffee
  • For the frosting:
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 2/3 cup cocoa powder
  • 3 cups powdered sugar
  • 1/3 cup milk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C)
  2. Grease and flour two 9-inch cake pans
  3. Mix all dry ingredients in a large bowl
  4. Add eggs, milk, oil, and vanilla
  5. Beat on medium speed for 2 minutes
  6. Stir in hot coffee
  7. Pour batter into prepared pans
  8. Bake for 30-35 minutes
  9. Cool completely before frosting
  10. Make frosting:
  11. - Melt butter
  12. - Stir in cocoa
  13. - Alternate adding sugar and milk
  14. - Beat until spreading consistency
  15. Frost cake and serve
