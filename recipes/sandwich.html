Classic Sandwich

Description

A delicious and easy-to-make classic sandwich perfect for lunch. This versatile recipe can be customized to your taste preferences.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread
  • 2-3 slices of your favorite deli meat (turkey, ham, or roast beef)
  • 1-2 slices of cheese
  • Lettuce leaves
  • Tomato slices
  • Mayonnaise
  • Mustard (optional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Toast the bread slices if desired
  2. Spread mayonnaise and mustard on both slices of bread
  3. Layer the bottom slice with lettuce leaves
  4. Add the deli meat
  5. Place cheese slices on top of the meat
  6. Add tomato slices
  7. Season with salt and pepper
  8. Top with the second slice of bread
  9. Cut diagonally and serve
