Fish Tacos

Description

Crispy fish tacos with fresh slaw and creamy sauce, perfect for a casual dinner.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb white fish fillets
  • 12 corn tortillas
  • 2 cups shredded cabbage
  • 1 cup Mexican crema
  • 1 lime, juiced
  • Seasoning for fish:
  • - Chili powder
  • - Cumin
  • - Garlic powder
  • Toppings:
  • - Cilantro
  • - Lime wedges
  • - Avocado

Steps

  1. Season fish with spices
  2. Make slaw with cabbage and lime
  3. Prepare cream sauce
  4. Cook fish until flaky
  5. Warm tortillas
  6. Assemble tacos
  7. Add toppings and serve
