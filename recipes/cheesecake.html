Classic New York Cheesecake

Description

A rich, creamy, and smooth New York-style cheesecake with a buttery graham cracker crust. This classic dessert is perfect for special occasions or when you're craving something truly indulgent!

Ingredients

  • For the Crust:
  • 2 cups graham cracker crumbs
  • 1/2 cup melted butter
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • For the Filling:
  • 4 (8 oz) packages cream cheese, softened
  • 1 1/4 cups sugar
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1/3 cup heavy cream
  • 1 tablespoon vanilla extract
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 2 tablespoons cornstarch
  • For the Topping (Optional):
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Fresh berries for garnish

Steps

  1. Prepare the Pan:
  2. Preheat oven to 325°F (165°C)
  3. Grease a 9-inch springform pan
  4. Wrap outside of pan with aluminum foil
  5. Make the Crust:
  6. Mix graham cracker crumbs, sugar, and salt
  7. Stir in melted butter until combined
  8. Press mixture into bottom and up sides of pan
  9. Bake for 10 minutes, then cool
  10. Make the Filling:
  11. Beat cream cheese until smooth
  12. Gradually add sugar, beating until blended
  13. Add eggs one at a time, beating well
  14. Mix in heavy cream and vanilla
  15. Add salt and cornstarch
  16. Pour into prepared crust
  17. Baking:
  18. Place pan in a larger roasting pan
  19. Fill roasting pan with hot water halfway up sides
  20. Bake for 1 hour and 15 minutes
  21. Turn off oven, crack door open
  22. Let cool in oven for 1 hour
  23. Optional Topping:
  24. Mix sour cream, sugar, and vanilla
  25. Spread over cooled cheesecake
  26. Refrigerate for at least 4 hours

Tips

  • Ensure all ingredients are at room temperature
  • Don't overmix after adding eggs
  • Use water bath to prevent cracking
  • Cool slowly to prevent sinking
  • Refrigerate overnight for best results
  • Run knife around edge before removing springform

Troubleshooting

  • If surface cracks: Cover with topping or fruit
  • If too dense: Ensure ingredients were room temperature
  • If crust is soggy: Double-wrap pan with foil
  • If browning too quickly: Cover with foil while baking

Storage

Store covered in refrigerator for up to 1 week. Can be frozen for up to 2 months.
