Classic Chili

Description

A hearty and spicy chili that's perfect for cold evenings or game day.

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs ground beef
  • 2 onions, diced
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 cans kidney beans
  • 2 cans diced tomatoes
  • 2 tablespoons chili powder
  • 1 tablespoon cumin
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • Optional toppings: cheese, sour cream, cilantro

Steps

  1. Brown ground beef in large pot
  2. Add onions and garlic
  3. Stir in spices
  4. Add beans and tomatoes
  5. Simmer for 1 hour
  6. Adjust seasonings to taste
  7. Serve with desired toppings
